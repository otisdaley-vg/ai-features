## What's new

The `/issues` archive page now ships with client-side topic filtering. Click a
chip at the top of the page to narrow the list. Click again to deselect.
Multiple chips compose with OR semantics — pick `astro` and `ux` and you'll
see issues tagged with either.

## Why no framework

Astro ships zero JavaScript by default. Adding React or Vue just for a chip
filter would mean hydrating a component tree to manage a `Set<string>`. The
whole interaction is ~30 lines of inline JS reading `data-topics` off each
row. The page stays static, the filter stays snappy.

## Tip

Use `data-active` rather than a class for toggle state on filter chips —
your stylesheet can target `[data-active]` without you having to remember to
add and remove a specific class, and the attribute serves as both the source
of truth and the styling hook.
